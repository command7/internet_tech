Internet vs Web

Internet

In 1958, the US Department of Defense(D0D) created Advanced Research Projects Agency(ARPA) which was funded to improve US military technology. One of the projects funded by ARPA was ARPANET which invented the first packet switching network and implemented TCP/IP protocols and laid the foundations of Internet. Internet can be described as a network of inter connected computers that is used as a basis for several other internet technologies such as Email, Telnet, etc.,

Web

Tim Berners Lee is considered to be the father of the Web. He invented the basic blocks of the web such as HTML, URI and HTTP. The web is a system of interconnected hyper text documents that are connected through the means of the internet.
